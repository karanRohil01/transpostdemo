Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. Here are some of the most common queries from our customers.

How do I get a quote for my move?

You can get a quote by filling out the contact form on our website or by calling us directly. We'll ask a few questions about your move to provide an accurate estimate.

Do you provide packing materials?

Yes, we provide high-quality packing materials including boxes, bubble wrap, packing tape, and more as part of our full-service packing option.

Is my stuff insured during the move?

Yes, we offer transit insurance to protect your belongings against any unforeseen circumstances during the move. Please discuss the options with our representative.

How much in advance should I book my move?

We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance, especially during peak moving seasons, to ensure availability and proper planning for your move.
